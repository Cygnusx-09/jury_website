Pash Studios

At Pash Studious, we believe pashmina is more than a fabric it is a story woven through generations, landscapes, and hands that carry the heritage of Jammu & Kashmir. Our studio was created with a vision to uplift and support the pashmina artisan community while bringing their craftsmanship to a wider contemporary audience.
